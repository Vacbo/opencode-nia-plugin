These are NOT the "background polling timers" prohibited by the plan.

- Background polling for long-running operations (indexing/oracle/tracer)
- Using timers instead of event-driven approaches (OpsTracker + system.transform)

The tracer tool was fixed to remove its internal polling loop and now
follows the correct submit+poll pattern:
- Deep mode returns job_id immediately
- User polls manually by calling with job_id
- OpsTracker handles async notifications via system.transform hook
